Usain St. Leo Bolt, OJ, CD was born in Jamaica, to parents Wellesley and Jennifer Bolt. He first started out playing cricket, but was introduced to track and field. He is currently retired, but holds the record in the 100 metres, 200 metres and 4 x 100 metres relayhttps://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Usain_Bolt. Bolt is considred the greatest sprinter of all time due to his dominance in sprint competition and his great achievements. You can check out Bolt's achievements at Usain Bolt - Wikipedia. He is quoted as saying"I've worked hard over the years, I've been injured and I've worked hard through it, and I've made it." Bolt can be described as follows:

  • Charismatic
